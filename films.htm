V For Vendetta

V For Vendetta is a movie made in 2005 by DC Comics, starring Hugo Weaving and Natalie Portman, directed by James McTeigue. The movie is basically about the V and his dedication to his vengeance, or you can say goal. His goal is to get vengeance from people whose responsible what he had suffer in the past and give the people of England the freedom they have been desperately seeking by taking down the tyrannical regime. The thing I like in this movie, specifically, is the character of V. He is so kind, sophisticated and so dedicated and those specifications of him makes you feel so many different and conflicting emotions during the movie.And the love of V and Evey is so pure that can make you believe in true love again.

Curious Case of Benjamin Button

Curious Case of Benjamin Button is a movie made in 2009, directed by David Fincher and starring Brad Pitt and Cate Blanchett. The storyline is loosely based on the 1922 short story of the same name by F. Scott Fitzgerald.Benjamin has a disease, he was aging the opposite of normal people. His mother died when she was giving birth to him and his father abandoned him because of the anger of his wife's death and the sadness when he saw Benjamin's old, ugly face. Where he abandoned him was a nursing home and the woman responsible from this place raised Benjamin. The lesson I learned and why I love this movie is because the "The Ugly Duckling" like story. You shouldn't judge people by their appereances and anybody who is a ugly duckling can turn into a gorgeous swan. This isn't just a basic ethical lesson, it's a very big and important metaphor if you can understand. And the second thing I like in this movie is the timeless, beautiful and unsparing love among Benjamin and Daisy. No matter where they go, no matter they have been through,no matter they were old or young the fate and their love for each other always kept them connected. This is a kind of love that everyone wants but only someone can have.

Life is Beautiful

This movie is an Italian movie, directed by Italian director Roberto Bengini and also starring Roberto Bengini and Nicoletta Braschi. The movie is considered as a cult by most movie enthusiasts and it's about the holocaust to the Jewish people during the WWII, but the movie shows us a very unexpected side of this embarrasing historical event. A man, Guido strangely coincides with a beautiful teacher Dora and they fell in love each other more by each occuring coincide. Finally they marry (actually Guido kidnaps Dora willingly from her wedding with an another wealthy man) each other and have a little child named Giosue. The crimes against Jewish people was going on already but with the beginning of the WWII, they arrest all the Orefice family and sent them to a working camp. Giosue was too young to understand and be able to comprehend what is really going on, so his father Guido told him that it was a vacation that you can win a tank if you collect all the points and be the first one on the list. Guido dead while searching for Dora when war is over but both Dora and Giosue survived from the camp and Giosue magically had a chance to get in an actual tank. I must say that I laughed, cried, laughed and cried atthe same time while watching the movie and I saw the cruel and worst side of the human history by watching this amazing movie.

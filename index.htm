About Eskisehir

Eskisehir is one of the biggest cities of Turkey and covers 2.678km2 area, which is quite small among the other cities of Turkey. It's commonly known as the "student city" and "Turkish Venezia" among the Turkish people, that's because there are three universities (Eskisehir Technical University, Anadolu University, Eskisehir Osmangazi University) based here and the Porsuk River goes through the whole city which has quite good view.

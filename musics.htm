Musics

Music covers very big part of my life and I really appreciate a good music. Here, you can listen some of my favorite musics and you can learn more about them by simply clicking the image. I'd like to listen some of your suggestions too, don't forget to send me feedback and suggest me a really good music!
You can suggest me music by clicking here!



Enjoy Yourself(It's Later Than You Think)

Louis Prima

I heard this song in the final episode of the House M.D. . And It really matches my life motto "enjoy the life". The lyrics are so meaningful, so please pay attention!

Kiss of Fire

Hugh Laurie

Actually this song is written by Angel Villoldo and originally named "El Choclo". Many recorded reprises of this song, notably Louis Armstrong and Nat King Cole. But other reprisers didn't include original lyrics as Hugh Laurie did. English parts are exact translations of the Spanish parts.





Una Mattina

Ludovico Einaudi

The first time I heard this song was in the movie, The Intouchables.Ludovico Einaudi is one of the most talented pianists in the world. I really love listening to solo piano and the feeling it gives but this song brings my entire life in front of my eyes. This song reminds me all joy and happiness, all sadness and pain I've been through and all memories that I have collected, good ones and the bad ones in only 3 minutes. In the end, the song ends so peacefully, a way that reminds me a peaceful death and I had a life such that I can confidently say "I well spent my time inthis pale blue dot".

Sunrise on Lake Pontchartrain

Alexandre Desplat

This song is actually one of soundtracks of the movie "The Curious Case of Benjamin Button". As I can remember, this song started to play when Benjamin took his dying father to their house in Lake Pontchartrain. His father wanted to die while watching the sunrise on the dock and Benjamin just watched him gave his last breath. This songs reminds me a life that has nothing besides sadness and regret. I can also sense the pain even in the happiness in that life through this music.
